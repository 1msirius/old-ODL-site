# Contributing to OpenDeepLearning

## Welcome Contributors 

Hi there! Thank you for your interest in contributing to OpenDeepLearning!

As an open-source community focused on making AI education accessible to all. We believe in the power of collaboration and welcome contributions from individuals with diverse backgrounds and expertise levels.

Your contributions, whether big or small, can significantly impact how we approach AI education and drive innovation in this field. By working together, we can create a comprehensive and inclusive learning platform that empowers learners worldwide.

Join our vibrant [community](https://discord.com/invite/bxnwugmNZg) and help shape the future of AI education through your collaborative efforts. Together, we can inspire and empower individuals to unlock their full potential in artificial intelligence.
